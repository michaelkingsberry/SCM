ABOUT | US

Who We Are

SelectCut Media, LLC is a full-service production company serving the Washington D.C. Metro Area. Our mission is to provide our clients with creative, high quality, full video production services on time and within budget. We strive to stay in touch with current trends in the industry in order to produce cutting-edge productions for our clients that stand out and tell the right story in the most creative way possible.

Our Skills

SelectCut Media brings a wealth of diverse skill sets to any multimedia production. Our skills range from initial concept development, script writing, on-site filming, non-linear editing, graphics and animation, and finally high-quality final mastered productions in the format requested by the client. We are very passionate about what we do, and we want our customers to be more than happy with the products we produce. We strive to drive creativity to the peak of excellence.
