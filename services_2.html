CORPORATE VIDEOS

A Corporate video is just as important as the company itself. A corporate video positively introduces the target audience to the company in a professional and creative manner by effectively using company branding. A corporate video can be used for educating the public on different products offered by the company; communicating the right message to staff, clients, or vendors; informing customers of new services offered by the company. SelectCut Media spends the time necessary to understand the company, its goals, and branding usage to produce a quality and successful corporate video that effectively delivers the message of the company.

PRODUCTION DEOMONSTRATION AND TRAINING VIDEOS

Training and product demonstration videos have become a household name in today’s multimedia world. Product demonstration and training videos enable companies to provide on-demand educational media to their staff. These videos can be streamed for live broadcast and archived online for continued viewing at the convenience of the staff. SelectCut Media can provide all product demonstration and training videos in any format including DVD, BluRay, and YouTube.

DOCUMENTARIES

Documentaries have come a long way since only being aired on the History channel. Now you can see documentaries on prime time television, on cable channels like HBO, and even in movie theaters. The purpose of a Documentary is to present a question or argument based on a certain realm of reality. As a result, the Documentary can educate, influence, or even become a controversial product of public opinion. In any case, SelectCut Media is here to meet your Documentary needs.

PSA VIDEOS

Public service advertisements (PSA) are important tools for educating the public and generating awareness on certain issues. PSAs are very short and concise video productions used to quickly and effectively make a point. There are three types of PSAs, network promotions; non-profit organizations; and cause-related marketing. Here at SelectCut Media, we look forward to producing any of these PSAs for your company because we feel they can make a difference and because we just love producing PSAs.
